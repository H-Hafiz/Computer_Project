Q) I'm having an issue with the online quiz, when will it be fixed?

If an issue is occuring, then our developers will work on fixing the problem as soon as they are aware of the problem. You may contact us to let us know of any issues and all updates will be sent to users and posted on all social media.

Q) I completed the game but didnt get a score.

An update is coming soon that will add a score system and a timer. Emails will be sent to every user on updates to the game or visit our social media pages to check for updates.

Q) I keep getting a 'bad Request' notification.

Try clearing your browser cookies, to clear cookies in chrome click on tools>browsing data>cookies>clear data. It may also be a problem with your internet connection, try restarting your router.

Q) Why isn't the flash version of the quiz working?

As of December 31,2020, Flash player will be completely removed from all browsers (chrome, edge, firefox etc). However updates with newer versions is playable for everyone with HTML5.
